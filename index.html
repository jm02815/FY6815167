Conference paper: Early Cosmology: From Prehistory to the Ancient Greece.

Abstract

“Humans have always contemplated the meaning of life and the Universe. 
 Mankind has always asked questions that are even asked today. 
 What is the purpose of life? How did Man originate? 
 What is the Universe and how did it begin? and how will it end. 
 The discipline which aims to unravel the origin and formation of the 
 universe is known as Cosmology. This short essay will illustrate early 
 cosmology, how humankind conceived the origin and formation of the Universe 
 in prehistoric times, to the first historical record, up until the time 
 of Hellenistic Greece. ”
